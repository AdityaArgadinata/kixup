## Real Madrid ready to break the bank for Michael Olise

The biggest story in Monday's transfer gossip is Real Madrid's reported interest in Bayern Munich forward Michael Olise. According to Spanish outlet Fichajes, the La Liga giants are preparing a deal that could reach €220 million (£188 million) for the France international.

Olise joined Bayern Munich from Crystal Palace in the summer of 2024 and has been outstanding since arriving in Germany. His combination of pace, creativity, and goal-scoring ability has made him one of the most exciting attackers in European football.

A move to Real Madrid would represent a massive statement of intent. The Spanish club are clearly looking to strengthen their forward line as they plan for the future, and Olise fits the profile of a Galáctico signing perfectly.

## Tottenham exploring a move for Rafael Leao

Tottenham Hotspur are reportedly exploring a move for AC Milan and Portugal forward Rafael Leao. According to Teamtalk, the 27-year-old is open to a switch to London, which could give Spurs a significant advantage in negotiations.

Leao has been one of Serie A's standout performers in recent seasons. His electric pace, dribbling skills, and ability to play across the front line make him an attractive target for any top club. Adding Leao to their attack would give Tottenham a genuine game-changer for the upcoming season.

## Arsenal and Liverpool chase Bradley Barcola

The race for Paris Saint-Germain forward Bradley Barcola is heating up. Both Arsenal and Liverpool are reportedly in the frame to sign the 23-year-old France international, whose future at PSG remains unclear.

Barcola has shown flashes of brilliance since joining PSG from Lyon. His pace, directness, and eye for goal have attracted attention from multiple Premier League clubs. If PSG decide to sell, a bidding war between Arsenal and Liverpool could push the price up significantly.

## Newcastle identify Angelo Stiller

Newcastle United have identified Stuttgart and Germany midfielder Angelo Stiller as the ideal candidate to strengthen their midfield. According to German outlet Bild, the 25-year-old is on the Magpies' radar as they look to build a squad capable of competing in the Champions League.

Stiller has been a key player for Stuttgart, impressing with his passing range, tactical intelligence, and ability to control the tempo of matches. His profile fits well with what Newcastle need — a technically gifted midfielder who can operate in a high-intensity system.

## Other notable gossip

Several other transfer stories are making headlines on Monday:

- **Manchester United** are eyeing Paraguay and San Lorenzo goalkeeper Orlando Gill, 26, as they look to strengthen between the posts (Fichajes).
- **Tottenham** also have Liverpool's Cody Gakpo and Manchester City's Savinho on their list of summer targets (Talksport).
- **Radu Dragusin** is set to leave Tottenham and join Fiorentina on an initial loan deal with an obligation to buy worth up to £21.4 million if conditions are met (Sky Sports).
- **Chelsea** defender Trevoh Chalobah, 26, would prefer a move to Inter Milan this summer (La Gazzetta dello Sport).
- **Trabzonspor** midfielder Christ Inao Oulai, 20, is being tracked by Juventus, Fiorentina, Brentford, Brighton, and RB Leipzig (La Gazzetta dello Sport).

## What to watch

The Olise saga is the one to keep an eye on this week. If Real Madrid follow through with a formal offer, it could trigger a domino effect across Europe — Bayern would need a replacement, and the flood of cash could see them enter the market for other targets.

For Tottenham, the links to Leao, Gakpo, and Savinho suggest the club are planning a major attacking overhaul this summer. Whether they can land any of these targets remains to be seen, but the ambition is clear.

Newcastle's pursuit of Stiller is also worth monitoring. The 25-year-old German midfielder has the qualities to thrive in the Premier League, and a move to St James' Park would represent a significant step up in his career.
