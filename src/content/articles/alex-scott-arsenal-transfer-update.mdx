Bournemouth have firmly rejected an enquiry from Arsenal for midfielder Alex Scott, making it clear the 22-year-old is not for sale this summer. The Cherries have drawn a line in the sand — and Arsenal will need to look elsewhere.

## What happened?

Arsenal made an approach to sign Alex Scott from Bournemouth during the summer transfer window. The Gunners identified Scott as a target to strengthen their midfield options ahead of the new season.

However, Bournemouth moved quickly to shut down any potential deal. The club told Arsenal that Scott is not available at any price, signalling their intent to keep hold of one of their most prized assets.

## Who is Alex Scott?

Alex Scott is a 22-year-old central midfielder who joined Bournemouth from Bristol City in 2023. He has developed into one of the most exciting young midfielders in the Premier League since making the step up from the Championship.

Scott is known for his technical ability, composure on the ball, and intelligent passing range. He typically operates as a number eight, contributing both in build-up play and in the final third. His performances have earned him recognition at England Under-21 level.

## Why do Arsenal want him?

Arsenal have been looking to add depth and quality to their midfield. Scott fits the profile Mikel Arteta values — a technically gifted player who can control tempo, press intelligently, and contribute going forward.

With Arsenal competing on multiple fronts including the Premier League and Champions League, having a deep and versatile squad is essential. Scott's age also makes him an attractive long-term investment.

## Why are Bournemouth refusing to sell?

Bournemouth have no financial pressure to sell Scott. The club has been building steadily under Andoni Iraola and view Scott as a key part of their project going forward.

Losing Scott would leave a significant hole in Bournemouth's midfield. The club paid a reported £25 million for him and would likely demand well above that fee — but their stance is simply that he is not for sale.

## What's next?

Arsenal will need to turn their attention to alternative targets. The Gunners have been linked with several midfielders this summer and will continue to explore options.

For Scott, the message is clear: Bournemouth see him as central to their ambitions. Whether a mega-offer could change their minds remains to be seen, but for now the door is firmly shut.

## What to watch

Keep an eye on whether Arsenal return with an improved approach or accept Bournemouth's stance and move on. Scott's future will likely depend on how much Bournemouth value him versus any potential offer — but right now, the Cherries are holding firm.
